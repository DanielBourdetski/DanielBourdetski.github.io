Tappo

Auto Tapper for Android

Tappo automates repetitive taps, swipes, and pinches on your Android device — an assistive tool for accessibility and hands-free repetitive tasks.

  • Place tap points anywhere on screen
  • Adjustable speed and run limits
  • Record & replay gestures (Pro)
  • Per-app profiles (Pro)
